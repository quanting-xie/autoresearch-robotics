Next attack — match the critic LR to the actor LR:
- LR_CRITIC 3e-4 -> 1e-3 (currently 3e-4, while actor is 1e-3 after exp7).
- Faster Q convergence -> actor receives sharper target gradients near goal boundary.
- Risk: critic instability. Mitigated by: twin Q networks, target net at tau=0.005, dense reward (so target is informative).

Predict: success_rate 0.6 -> 0.7-0.8. If the symmetric LR helps, exp 9 might bump tau too. If it diverges, fall back to LR_CRITIC=5e-4 in exp 9.
